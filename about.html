About Jiya

Meet Jiya

Creative strategist, MA Luxury and Brand Management student at SCAD, and a global thinker with a personal lens on culture, commerce, and luxury experience.

Point of view

Luxury strategy, with a life behind it.

I am a creative strategist pursuing my MA in Luxury and Brand Management at SCAD. With a focus on merchandising, buying, and cultural storytelling, I bring together academic insight, hands-on internships, and museum work to shape how luxury brands connect with people.

Originally from Rajasthan, born in Gujarat, and raised in New Delhi, I have also lived in Sri Lanka, Qatar, Israel, and now the United States. Moving through these cultures has given me a perspective rooted in empathy, adaptability, and global curiosity.

Outside of work and school, I am a marathon finisher in Tel Aviv and a big fan of live music. I also love cooking, reading, and staying active. At my core, I like to call it Barbie in the boardroom: polished, warm, ambitious, and ready to do the work.
